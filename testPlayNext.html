All the gifts we make, like the Amabie 'Avente' gift set (shown on the illustration above), can only be ordered in an individually tailored fashion. You get to choose the color, size, hardware, & more — all according to your taste.

Our collections are extremely limited: we never make the same gift twice. Let that sink in: your gift will be the only such gift in the world.

We use Palmellato leather sourced from Antiba, an Italian tannery known for its exceptional quality. This leather is highly resistant to scratches, dirt, stains, & water damage — all while offering a luxurious touch and appearance. It undergoes vegetable tanning for an excellent scent & environmentally-friendly production process. The full-grain leather we use is considered the epitome of grandeur – it's richly textured & opulent.

Every piece is meticulously crafted by the skilled Amabie artisans who pour their souls into making the absolute top-notch gifts.
